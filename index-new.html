    East Africa Bank: 90-Day Compliance Sprint

    Financial Services | Sub-Saharan Africa

    Challenge

    Legacy infrastructure with 47 critical regulatory violations. National banking authority required remediation in 120 days or operational license suspension.

    Solution

    Comprehensive audit mapped all violations to local regulations. Prioritized 12 critical fixes. Executed 90-day remediation sprint with daily standups and weekly executive briefings.

    Results
    • 47 violations → 3 (audit closure within 90 days)

    • Ongoing quarterly monitoring contract initiated
